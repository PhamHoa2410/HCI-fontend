Top 10 Best Apps for Android – Free Apps 2019 (May)

    `
  • Review
  • 03 Comments



In this video we are going to show you Top 10 Best Mobile Apps for May 2019. Some of these apps are recent launches and some are there on Android Play Store for quite some time. We have Handpicked these apps on the basis of the functionality, uniqueness and usability.

App List

1) Trebel Music

TREBEL is the only music downloader app for Android that lets you download free songs and albums for free and play them offline. Listen to music without WiFi anywhere while saving battery life and data with our free music player app.



2) Daywise

Whether you’re a student or a working professional, Daywise will help you develop the focus and self-control to start performing at higher levels. Soon, you’ll start feeling more relaxed, more in control of your time and ready to win the day!




3) Energy Ring

Adds a configurable Energy Ring around the camera lens indicating current Battery level. Dive into the various configuration options, not only you can quickly glance and get the battery info but, Energy Ring adds an accent to your phone’s camera lens.




4) Everpics Wallpaper

Everpics contains hand picked high quality wallpapers with auto wallpaper changer ability. Use the auto wallpaper changer feature, to change your mobile wallpaper through your own favorite pictures automaticly.




5) Flashdog – GFX For PUBG Mobile

FlashDog is a game must-have app, providing diverse and powerful game supported features to make game with more fun, and our main purpose is to support our users for better gaming experiences. We help you to run your games faster and smoother, and provide you with a safe and private environment while you are gaming.




Insta Story

Insta Story is an ig story maker that has dozens of well-design templates and layouts for you to create beautiful stories to share. Let’s unfold your Insta Story!




7) Keypress Call Accept for WhatsApp

Keypress Call Accept allows you to accept/decline an incoming WhatsApp call by pressing one of the physical buttons on your device. This can be the volume buttons, or the Home and Back key (optional). No more swiping to accept/decline a call!




8) Laughable

The world’s best-rated podcast app for iPhone has come to Android, at long last! Laughable is paradise for podcast lovers & comedy fans – there’s nothing else like it, and it’s completely free.




9) Lightbeam

Make any app collaborative with lightbeam’s social screen sharing – whether its swiping for a date, shopping for an outfit, or booking an Airbnb. Happy sharing!




Firefox send

Firefox Send lets you share files with end-to-end encryption and a link that utomatically expires. So you can keep what you share private and make sure your stuff doesn’t stay online forever.
